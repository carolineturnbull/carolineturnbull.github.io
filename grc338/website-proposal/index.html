Introduction

Leo Boutique

My company is a clothing and home goods shop called Leo. It is focused on selling beautiful, sustainable clothing and home goods.

Target audience

The target audience is young women interested in trends and sustainability. They enjoy shopping online and discovering new, unique, and well-designed products.

Their primary goals are to find a curated selection of cool, beautiful objects for their homes, closets, and to give as gifts. They would visit this site for shopping purposes, or to find out more about the store before shopping in-person.

About

Leo is an intentionally sourced storefront carrying sustainable and handmade goods. Our store is in the heart of Downtown Los Angeles. We value brilliant design, quality, and supporting artists. In our store you will find a wide selection of handmade and vintage clothing, art, home goods, and gifts.
